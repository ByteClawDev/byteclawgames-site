The Haunted Loop

Multiplayer Psychological Horror

Break the Cycle. Survive the Anomalies.

The Haunted Loop is a psychological horror experience built in Unreal Engine 5. You and your friends are trapped in a recursive reality—a loop that degrades with every cycle.

Navigating through 7 terrifying chapters, you must identify anomalies, solve puzzles, and survive encounters with entities that defy logic. Inspired by the depth of SCP folklore, every boss encounter requires more than just firepower—it requires observation and sanity.

Key Features

  • Co-op Madness: Work together to spot anomalies, or succumb to paranoia alone.
  • SCP-Inspired Entities: Face off against complex AI bosses like the shadow-manipulating SCP-122.
  • Degrading Reality: The environment changes with every loop. Trust nothing.
  • Unreal Engine 5: Hyper-realistic lighting and atmosphere that gets under your skin.
